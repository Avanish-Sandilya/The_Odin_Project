Pesto Pasta

Ingredients
  • 1 package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Half cup chopped onions
  • 2 and a half tablespoons pesto or more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain; transfer into a large bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in pesto, salt, and black pepper until warmed through.
  5. Stir pesto mixture into hot pasta; stir in grated cheese to coat.
